How to use?

Start by exploring the comprehensive learning materials and instructional videos to build a solid understanding of each topic. Once you're ready, put your knowledge to the test by completing the interactive quizzes to track your progress and master the subject.

01

Register

Register by entering your Email, Username, and a secure Password. Don't forget to include your Student ID (NIM) to verify your account and access all features.

02

Login

To access your dashboard, simply enter your registered Email along with your Password. Once verified, you'll be instantly reconnected to your lessons, saved progress, and upcoming quizzes.

03

Explore

Browse our Menu of videos and materials using the search and filter tools. Quickly find any topic you need and jump straight into new lessons or quizzes.

04

Learning Materials

Access a curated collection of structured lessons and reading modules designed for deep understanding. Each material is paired with key takeaways to help you master core concepts at your own pace.

05

Start Quiz

Put your knowledge to the test by launching our interactive assessments to receive instant feedback on your performance. Track your scores and identify key areas for improvement as you work toward mastering each subject.

06

Learning Videos

Watch our high-quality tutorials and visual guides to simplify complex topics through engaging explanations. You can play, pause, and revisit any lesson to ensure you grasp every detail at your own speed.
